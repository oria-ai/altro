# Entity decision — עמותה / חברה / חל"צ

**This is the blocking decision.** Every application (בית תוכנה, Meta,
privacy filings, Kol Zchut) wants a registered entity behind it.

## The three options

### A. עמותה (nonprofit association)
- **For:** mission-aligned ("People and AI For Good"); eligible for
  philanthropy/federation/ministry funding — which is exactly who pays for
  face B (copilot for social workers); clean fit with Kol Zchut's
  NonCommercial license; public trust (critical when asking people for
  life stories).
- **Against:** needs ≥2 founders; רשם העמותות approval is slow (weeks-months);
  אישור ניהול תקין (needed for most institutional funding) takes ~2 years of
  operating history; can't take investment; salaries fine but no equity upside.

### B. חברה בע"מ (Ltd company)
- **For:** registered online in days; can pivot, take investment, pay
  founders; simplest ops.
- **Against:** Kol Zchut content becomes off-limits without a negotiated
  license; "AI company monetizing the poor's benefits" is a trust and PR
  problem the whole plan (07) warns about; closes most philanthropy doors.

### C. חל"צ (public-benefit company)
- **For:** company-speed registration with a locked public purpose; no
  dividends (profits stay in the mission) — keeps the "never % of a kitzba"
  line structurally; more credible to Kol Zchut/ministries than a plain Ltd.
- **Against:** still not a nonprofit for some funders' purposes; conversion
  out is hard; less familiar to tech partners.

## Lean

**A (עמותה)** if the strategy is face B / grant-funded — the funders, the
license, and the trust story all line up. **C (חל"צ)** if you want
company-speed and operational freedom while keeping the ethical lock-in.
Plain B only if you've decided this is a venture-backed product company —
which contradicts 07's hard line as currently written.

A pragmatic middle: register the **עמותה now with 2 founders** (you + one
co-founder you trust), start the ניהול-תקין clock, and let a commercial arm
be a later decision if ever needed.

## What registration takes (whoever is chosen)

- עמותה: רשם העמותות online filing — name ×3 alternatives, purposes (מטרות),
  2+ founders' details + signatures, ~₪900 fee. Purposes text drafted below.
- חברה/חל"צ: רשם החברות online — name, purposes, director, ~₪1,200 fee,
  same-week registration.

## Draft purposes (מטרות) text — usable for either form

> קידום מיצוי זכויות חברתיות וכלכליות בישראל באמצעות טכנולוגיה ובינה
> מלאכותית; הנגשת מידע על זכויות לציבור, ובפרט לאוכלוסיות מוחלשות;
> פיתוח והפעלת כלים טכנולוגיים המסייעים לפרט ולגורמי מקצוע לאתר, להבין
> ולממש זכויות מול רשויות המדינה וגופים ציבוריים; קידום שיתופי פעולה עם
> גופי ממשל, חברה אזרחית ואקדמיה בתחום זה.
