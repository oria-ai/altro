# 04 — Product directions

Same engine (03), four possible faces. Not mutually exclusive long-term, but
the MVP must pick one.

## A. Direct-to-person conversational agent (B2C)

WhatsApp-first (the population's actual channel; voice notes are native there
and voice is where wording+tone signal lives), web as secondary. Person talks,
photographs documents, gets gap report + executed claims.

- **For:** purest expression of the thesis; largest reach; the demo is
  magical ("I told it about אמא and it found ₪3,400/חודש").
- **Against:** trust cold-start (people don't hand life stories to unknown
  bots), safety/regulatory surface is maximal, monetization is ethically
  loaded (charging the poor % of their kitzba = becoming the predators of 02).

## B. Copilot for the human מיצוי layer (B2B2C)

The tool of עובדות סוציאליות, מרכזי עוצמה, יד מכוונת, hospital social workers,
nonprofit hotlines. The professional talks with the person (or pastes/records
the conversation, with consent); the engine extracts, diffs, pre-fills; the
human reviews and submits.

- **For:** trust and distribution inherited from the human; human-in-the-loop
  neutralizes the legal-advice and hallucination risks (07); these workers are
  drowning and tooled with nothing; funders (federations, ministries,
  philanthropy) pay for exactly this.
- **Against:** sales cycle to institutions; the "person brings their own
  wording/tone" signal arrives second-hand; smaller TAM.

## C. Embedded engine / API

License the diff engine to whoever already has the users: kupot holim,
municipalities (מרכזי עוצמה), banks, insurers, even mybenefits itself.

- **For:** scale without owning trust; the state as customer instead of
  competitor.
- **Against:** longest cycle, deepest procurement pain, thesis diluted to
  whatever the host's UX allows.

## D. Wedge: single high-pain vertical, full-stack

Pick ONE right-cluster with high value, clear rules, digital filing, and
documented under-claiming — candidates: **מענק עבודה** (negative income tax:
simple rules, pure money, known ~30-40% non-take-up, annual deadline),
**caregiver/סיעוד bundle**, **post-miluim bundle** (timely, funded, sympathetic),
**tax refunds** (proven by Finupp et al. — but crowded). Do discovery →
execution end-to-end for that one cluster, then expand.

- **For:** smallest honest scope; measurable ("we recovered ₪X"); the
  commercial comparables prove payment.
- **Against:** narrow wedge can look like "another refund app" and lose the
  big-thesis identity.

## Current lean (to pressure-test)

**D-inside-B or D-inside-A:** wedge vertical for focus, with the
person-as-input engine (03) as the architecture from day one so expansion is
adding rules, not rebuilding. Decision criteria + open questions in 08.
Nonprofit vs. for-profit vs. hybrid is a real fork — see 07/08.
